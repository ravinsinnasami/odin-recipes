Poori Recipe

Description

A filling and delicious breakfast. Can be made very quickly. Eat it with curry or potato masala.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon fine rava (semolina or suji)
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • 1/4 cup water

Steps to make Poori:

  1. Add 1 cup wheat floor, 3/4 teaspoon fine rava and 1/8 teaspoon salt to a bowl.
  2. Add in 3/4 teaspoon oil and mix well. Add in 1/4 cup of water and mix
  3. Once dough forms, add in 1-2 tablespoons of water. Continue kneading until a stiff dough is formed
  4. Smoothen the dough into a ball shape using a little of oil.
  5. Cover dough and let rest for 3-5 minutes.
  6. Divide the dough into 8-10 parts and roll into smooth balls.
  7. Sprinkle flour on counter and flatten the dough slightly. Use a rolling pin to roll the dough evenly.
  8. Add oil to frying pan and heat it using medium heat.
  9. Add a small dough to see if it rises. If it does, the oil is hot enough.
  10. Add dough into the oil. Let it fry and float up (at least halfway to surface).
  11. Gently press down poori. Once it completely poofs up, flip poori over and let it fry until golden.
  12. Remove poori using strainer and put on paper towel.
  13. Serve poori with curry or masala.
