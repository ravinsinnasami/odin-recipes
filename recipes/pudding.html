Pudding Recipe

Description

A South Indian dessert that is made using vermicelli. It is often served during festivals or celebrations

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups of vermicelli
  • 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of green cardamom powder
  • 4 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoon of ghee
  • cashew nuts and raisins as needed.

Steps to make Recipe:

  1. Add 1 tablespoon of ghee into pan. Once heated, add cashew nuts and fry till golden.
  2. Add in some raisins and stir fry them. Once done, remove all onto a plate.
  3. Once again add 1 tablespoon of ghee to the pan and add 3/4 cups of semiya.
  4. Roast the semiya until it turns slightly golden brown.
  5. Next add in the milk into the semiya and bring it to a boil at low to medium heat.
  6. Continuously stir to prevent from burning. Remove the pudding from pot once the vermicelli is cooked
  7. Serve the pudding in a bowl or cup and add the toasted cashew nuts and raisins to it.
