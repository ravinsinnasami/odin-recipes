Biryani Recipe

Description

A South Indian dish that can be made with meat or just plain. Suitable for both lunch and dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Cups of rice
  • 3.5 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1/2 cup of sliced onion
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger paste
  • 1/2 cup of cut tomato
  • 1/4 cup of curd
  • handful of mint
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon of biryani masala powder

Steps to make Recipe:

  1. Wash and soak the rice. Drain after 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare onion and tomatoes.
  3. Add oil into pressure cooker and add spices. Saute until brown.
  4. Add sliced onions and saute till brown.
  5. Add ginger paste and saute till fragrant.
  6. Add tomato along with chili powder, tumeric and biryani masala. Cook until tomato turns mushy
  7. Add water and bring it to a boil. Once boil, add drained rice to oil. Cook in pressure cooker for 5 to 6 minutes.
